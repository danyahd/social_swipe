Social Swipe Extension

TL;DR: Social Swipe stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your data. Everything stays on your computer.

1. Information We Collect

Social Swipe collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Social media posts you choose to save (including text, images, author information, and URLs)
  • Tags you add to organize your saved posts
  • Your extension settings and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

All collected information is used solely for:

  • Displaying your saved posts in the extension popup
  • Organizing and searching through your saved content
  • Providing export/import functionality for backups

3. Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API (chrome.storage.local). This means:

  • ‚úÖ Data stays on your computer only
  • ‚úÖ No servers, no cloud storage, no external databases
  • ‚úÖ No one can access your data except you
  • ‚úÖ Data is not synced across devices

4. Data We DON'T Collect

Social Swipe does NOT collect, track, or transmit:

  • ‚ùå Your browsing history
  • ‚ùå Personal identifying information
  • ‚ùå Analytics or usage statistics
  • ‚ùå Cookies or tracking data
  • ‚ùå Any data to third-party servers

5. Permissions Explanation

Social Swipe requests the following permissions:

activeTab

Required to add save buttons to posts on social media websites you visit.

storage

Required to save your posts locally on your device.

scripting

Required to inject the save button functionality into social media pages.

Host Permissions

Access to Twitter/X, Instagram, Reddit, and YouTube is required to add save buttons on these platforms. We only interact with these sites to add the save functionality.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to anyone. Period.

Your saved posts never leave your device unless you explicitly export them yourself.

7. Third-Party Services

Social Swipe does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download all your data as a JSON file anytime
  • Delete: Clear individual posts or all data from settings
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension deletes all local data

9. Children's Privacy

Social Swipe does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is intended for general audiences.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, you can reach us at:

  • Website: socialswipe.online
  • Chrome Web Store: Social Swipe extension page
Summary: Social Swipe is a privacy-first extension. All your data stays on your device, and we don't collect or transmit anything to external servers.
